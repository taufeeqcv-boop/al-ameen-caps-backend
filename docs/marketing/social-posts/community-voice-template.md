## Visual strategy (Gold & Emerald)

When you post, use these three tips to maximize impact:

1. **The visual**  
   Use a high-resolution photo of the product mentioned in the review (e.g. the Na'lain Cap). If possible, photograph it against a backdrop that evokes Bo-Kaap â€” vibrant colors or traditional textures.

2. **The stars**  
   Use **Emerald Green** or **Muted Gold** star icons as an overlay on the image. This creates "visual scent": the same star styling customers see on the website (Featured Reviews section) appears on Facebook, reinforcing brand consistency.  
   - Gold: brand accent `#D4AF37` (default site stars).  
   - Emerald: deep green `text-emerald-800` / `fill-emerald-800` (homepage variant).

3. **The tag**  
   If the customer who left the review has a public profile, tag them in the comments with "Shukran again!" This often surfaces the post to their friends and expands reach organically.
